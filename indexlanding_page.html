Around Motown


Project Description


The goal of this project is to create avenues of public engagement with Motown Museum Collections.
Currently, the Motown Museum website does not have a page to display digitizations of their collections. In an effort to help more people engage with Motown collections we will create a page on the current website to host curated audio and digital collections, and create QR code decals that will be placed around the city. This way, the Detroit visitors and locals who are unfamiliar with Motown collections can encounter them as they walk around Detroit, creating a digital experience.

Authors: Morgan, Emily, and Angélica
